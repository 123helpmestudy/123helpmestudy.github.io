About Us

Hello, my name is Marcel, founder of 123 Help Me Study. Let me be the first to welcome you to this website. I started tutoring in the UK in 2017 and immediately found that I enjoyed giving the knowledge I gained from my GCSE, A Level and university studies, back to my students. For me it was a academic outlet that could not be achieved in the workplace.

I setup 123 Help Me Study to allow tutors to start their tutoring journeys, as I did, and keep 100% of their listed rate. The learning process should involve two fundamental parts, a student wanting to learn and an able tutor to guide the student through the learning process. All other aspects are secondary, and this is what we want to facilitate on behalf of the tutors.

While I have been tutoring I have come to learn that parents always want the best for their children. This message has stuck with me, and because of this we offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our lessons. We achieve this by vetting tutors and offering a money back guarantee. We strongly believe that education is one of the core pillars of a prosperous society, therefore we want to do our part and make the educational process as smooth as possible.

Are you ready to get started? Head on over to our subjects page and start the process of finding a tutor to suit you. We are always on hand to help you through the process, simply call or message us.
