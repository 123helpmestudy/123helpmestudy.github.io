How we work

Many of the tutors that run and help 123 Help Me Study have been tutoring for over 5 years. We pride ourselves on building strong lasting relationships with the families we help. We do this becuase we want our students to succeed.

This platform is designed to allow parents, students and tutors to sign-up and manage their lessons effectively, taking all the background work and admin away, leaving students and tutors to focus on learning. The best part is, the affordable price you see, is the price you pay!

On our system you will be able to view all tutors who can help build your confidence in a subject you need. Likewise, tutors can view posts of students who haven't been paired with a subject-matter expert yet.

You can view all your upcoming appointments and how much each appointment will cost you. You can at all times speak with as many tutors you wish, in order for you to find the perfect match for you. Should you not be able to find what you need, do not hesitate to call us, our team is always on hand to help.
