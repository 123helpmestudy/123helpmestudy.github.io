How does this platform work?
Answer: Tutors are required to fill in all necessary information to create a complete and accurate profile that gets submitted for approval to the 123 Help Me Study tutor-support team. Please remember this information will be freely available to any potential students or parents coming to the website. We will never display your personal details on this website, therefore we ask that you also do not put these in your open profile.
How do I get paid?
Answer: First, you will need to go to the "Tutor Profile" page (found in your homepage when you log in) and fill in all the required details. Second, once you have been approved, your profile page will be live on this website. This is when students & parents will start messaging you. Third, respond to requests and build an initial relationship with your new customers. Once you are happy to proceed, book in your first lesson. Fourth, and lastly, your customers will approve the lesson details and make a payment to 123 Help Me Study. By giving lessons to students you earn money.
When do I get paid?
Answer: We allow customers 24 hours to give feedback on their lessons with you. 24 hours after the lesson takes place we send you the money you earned to your desired payment method. Please remember that we can only make payments to authorised account holders. This requires you to go to your "Account" page (found in your homepage when you log in) and upload a valid proof of identity.
